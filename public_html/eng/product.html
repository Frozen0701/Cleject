PLEASE NOTE, that in order to unify and simplify the operation of chlorinators produced by Chlorejection Technologies LLC, we are doing systematic work on the transfer of new products to the generally accepted standard for threaded connections G3/4 instead of M27*1.5. We also manufacture spare parts and chlorinators with regular threads. You may choose the required thread at order. АХВ-1000/Р12-КЛ is designed for ejection dosing of chlorine gas and of other gases - with minimal modifications. It consists of an ejector M260C with a hydraulic valve and a dose-meter which includes a shut-off valve, a vacuum regulator, a filter, a pressure gauge, a control valve and a rotameter. A second control valve can be installed at the request of the customer. Chlorine flow control is carried out manually by means of a control valve. Dosing starts when there is ejection and stops when there is no ejection. The chlorinator is mounted on a unified panel, which is fixed on the wall of the chlorinator. One of the design modifications of the chlorinator AXB-1000/Р12-КЛ is used for water disinfection with sodium hypochlorite solution. In this modification, the ejector does not contain a hydraulic valve, and the dose-meter has no filter, pressure gauge and vacuum regulator. Mounting and start-up of these chlorinators create no problems, but must be carried out by qualified personnel to ensure the reliable and safe operation of the chlorination system. Authorized maintenance of the device must be performed once a year. Dosing accuracy is 2.5% of the set value. Adjustment ratio 1:20. Net weight: no more than 20kg. Gross weight: not more than 40kg.
